\subsubsection{\texorpdfstring{initialize()}{initialize()}}
{\footnotesize\ttfamily void initialize (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{void}]{ }\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Runs initialization code. 

Creates an emulation of the three-\/button, U\+A\+R\+T-\/based V\+EX L\+CD on the display.

This occurs as soon as the program is started.

All other competition modes are blocked by initialize; it is recommended to keep execution time for this mode under a few seconds.
